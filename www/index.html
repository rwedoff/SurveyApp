How to Use CAB Tablets

  1. Unlock the box
  1. There are 3 tablets, one in the Iowa Theatre Office, one in the IL Room Projector Room, and in the CAB Office.
  2. Obtain the keys for each room, for
    CAB Roaming (In the CAB Office): 48
    CAB Iowa Theatre: 55
    CAB IL Room:  52

  1. Use the Lock Box key on each keyring to unlock the box.
  2. Unplug and take the tablet out of the box.

  1. Unlock the Tablet
  1. Use the side button to wake the tablet.
  2. ONCE YOU UNLOCK THE TABLET YOU MUST CHECK IT OUT USING THE GOOGLE FORM.
  3. **If leaving the IMU, Checkout the tablet in the IMU where there is internet!
  4. Leave the volume off. Every time a survey is filled out, all three devices will ring if sound is not turned off.
  1. Homescreen:
  1. On the homescreen you will find:
  1. Tablet Checkout Survey:  Information below.
  2. Loop Survey App:  Information below.
  3. Youtube:  Use this for music and advertising purposes
  4. Camera:  Feel free to take pictures and post them in the story.  Please save every picture and video to device, photos are automatically backed up to Google Photos Server.
  5. SnapChat & Instagram:  Feel free to take pictures and post them in the story.  Please save every picture and video to device!
  6. QR Codes, scan the QR code with Snapchat (to add CAB to Snapchat)  or another QR code reader, like Google Goggles (to add CAB on Facebook)
  1. How to Checkout a Tablet
  1. Click on the Tablet Checkout Form on the home screen
  2. Fill out survey with name, date, time, location, and reason for using the tablet, all questions are required!
  1. This form is a contract leaving the exec member responsible for anything that happens to the device while it is in use.  At any time the device can be locked, located, or rung by a device administrator.
  1. How to administer surveys
  1. Open the CAB Survey App.
  2. Internet is not required!
  3. Tap on the plus sign to create a new survey or open the menu to see already made surveys.
  4. Tap on the Survey for the Event you would like to collect data for. Surveys will be created for every event and movie.
  5. The survey is automatically locked.  The password to unlock the survey is “entertainment”.
  6. Give survey to people.  If the tablet is vertical, the whole survey is there, if horizontal, swipe right to get to the next question.
  7. Press “submit” after each survey.

  1. Putting Tablet Away:
  1. Close all apps on tablet, leave it turned on and make sure Wifi and GPS are still on.  To do this, press the button next to the home button and press “Close all apps”
  2. Clear all the notifications in the notification bar.  To access the notification bar, swipe from the top of the screen down.  Press the blue X to clear all notifications.
  3. Place the tablet in the lockbox.
  4. Plug the tablet into the charging cord in the lockbox.
  5. Lock the box
  6. Make sure the lockbox is attached to something else.
  1. General Rules When Using Tablets
  1. The tablet should not be used for any reason except for official CAB Events or Marketing reasons.  Messing around with them in the CAB Office is not permitted.
  2. When not in use, the CAB Tablets should be locked and stored in their proper locations per the checkout form.
  3. The person who checks out the tablet is responsible for anything that happens to it.
  4. Refrain from downloading apps and unnecessary programs on the tablets.
  5. Do not take the tablet out of the case.
  6. When held by someone else, keep an eye on where the tablet is. Tablets can be located with Android Device Manager.  Call Ryan immediately if lost.
  7. If any questions, concerns, or troubleshooting call Ryan Wedoff:

 (847) 452-2847
